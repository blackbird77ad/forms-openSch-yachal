About Open School of Ministry

Our local church, Saints Community Church, runs Livingword Ministerial Academy (L.M.A) as an internal leadership training school for church leaders. Open School of Ministry is our broader open class for church members and non-church members.

The Open School of Ministry first held in July 2015, with three subsequent editions and the latest in July 2024. The fifth edition will hold from Monday, July 6th – Wednesday, July 8th, 2026, with arrival on Sunday, July 5th, 2026.

Registration deadline: Sunday, June 28, 2026.

This registration page is only for the Ghana approved center at Yachal House, Ridge Accra. If you are outside Ghana, please visit https://osom.saintscommunity.net/ for other participation options.
